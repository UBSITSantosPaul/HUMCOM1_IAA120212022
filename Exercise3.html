How To Repair A Computer

  1. Backup your files.
  2. Give your computer a bit of test.
  3. Update your drivers.
  4. Uninstall programs that might be causing the issue.
  5. Inspect your device drivers.
  6. Check your CPU and memory storage.
  7. Determine if your computer is overheating.
  8. Run a diagnostic check.
  9. Install and run a good antivirus and anti-malware program.
  10. Try a system restore.
  11. Reinstall your operating system.

How To Cook Italian Pasta

  1. Boil Water
    • Start with a very large pot of water, about 6 quarts per pound of pasta. Bring it to a rapid boil. Tip: A pound of pasta feeds about 5 to 6 people.

  2. Add Salt
    • Put in a lot of salt, about 3 tablespoons. If you taste the water, it should taste like the sea.

  3. Add the Pasta
    • Drop in the pasta while the water is at a boil and give it a gentle stir.

  4. Stir
    • Stir it occasionally while it cooks. Use the cooking time on the pasta package as a guide.

  5. Taste the Pasta
    • Sample the pasta at about 2 minutes shy of the indicated time.

  6. Drain
    • Drain the pasta. Just be sure to save about 1 cup of the pasta water.

  7. Removing Ravioli
    • If you're cooking ravioli, use a slotted skimmer and gently lift the ravioli a few at a time.

  8. Stir in the Sauce
    • Add the pasta to your sauce and finish cooking it in the sauce. Give it a stir to coat.

  9. Add Pasta Water
    • Ladle a few tablespoons of the pasta water at a time until you have the right consistency to your sauce. Remember, the pasta will absorb the liquid.

  10. Buon Appetito!
    • Simmer over medium-low heat.
